1. What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

The first element that catched my eyes was the duration of time on the loading page. I noticed that it took about 10 seconds to load the page. It also had a timer run to 100 with a website’s logo in the middle of the left corner. In addition, this website requires eye-catching and high interaction with users. Along with 3D graphics, an easy-to-see and gentle pastel tones background was a direct advantage. It created a sense of gentle harmony, highlighting the dominant object and the identification for the company brand.

2. Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions.

  • Move the mouse around the main object of the website to see how it changes the perspective (3D environments)
  • Leave the mouse in front of the exterior floating next to the main object to display the table of contents.
  • Scroll down to see each section of context.
  • Click on everything until the mouse turns into a hand.
  • Click on an arrow box that displays the name of the content.
  • Move the mouse left and right to see how every floating frame of the table of contents changes direction.
